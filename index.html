Our Mission: Bridging the Language Gap in Tech

Tech Lingo was founded in 2022 by a team of language educators and software engineers who saw firsthand how language barriers were holding back talented tech professionals.

We believe that technical skills shouldn't be limited by language proficiency. Our mission is to empower non-native English speakers in tech to communicate effectively, advance their careers, and contribute fully to the global tech community.
